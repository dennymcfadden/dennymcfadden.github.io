Thanks for stopping by

I’ve always been a maker. As a child, my mom would send me out with a brush and a bucket of water to “paint” the sidewalk and I would be absorbed for hours. In high school, a a legendary art teacher sparked my love for drawing, which led to a BFA in Graphic Design. But it was a undergraduate HTML/CSS course—coinciding with the launch of the first iPhone—that instilled a passion for software as a medium. For the past 15 years, I’ve been building digital products at early-stage startups—using design to make complex things simple and delightful.

Outside of work I pursue interests that combine teamwork, craft, and technology. Today you can find me:

  • Competitive sailing (2024 BCYA Champions)
  • Playing team sports: basketball, soccer, and ice hockey
  • Drawing & painting at MICA (Maryland Institute College of Art)
  • Restoring our family’s 1894 townhome
